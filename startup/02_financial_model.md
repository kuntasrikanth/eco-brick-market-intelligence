# Financial Model — TerraBlock Mud Interlocking Brick Factory
### 3-Year Projection | Telangana | 2026–2028

> **Basis:** Product is the **Compressed Stabilised Earth Block (CSEB)**, 300 × 150 × 90 mm — one block replaces ~2 conventional bricks plus mortar.
> Pricing anchored to the **real market range of ₹30–50 per block** (Brick&Bolt, 2026); we enter below it at ₹28.
> These figures supersede the earlier ₹11/brick draft and are aligned with the investor deck.

---

## Assumptions

| Assumption | Value | Basis |
|------------|-------|-------|
| Product | CSEB block, 300×150×90 mm | Standard interlocking profile |
| Selling price (Y1–Y2) | ₹28 / block | Below market range ₹30–50 |
| Selling price (Y3) | ₹30 / block | Certified, brand established |
| Plant capacity | 2,500 blocks/day (2 machines) | ~1,250/machine/shift |
| Capacity (Year 3) | 5,000 blocks/day (4 machines) | +2 machines added in Y3 |
| Working days | 280 (Y1), 300 (Y2–3) | Monsoon + maintenance allowance |
| Utilisation | Y1 ramp · 70% (Y2) · 65% (Y3, new capacity) | Conservative |
| COGS per block | ₹15.0 | Material ₹8.5 + direct labour ₹3.5 + power/overhead ₹3.0 |
| Gross margin | ~46% (at ₹28) · ~50% (at ₹30) | Soil is near-zero-cost raw material |
| Tax rate | 25% | MSME company rate |

**Why Year 1 is a loss:** the plant takes ~6 months to set up (civil work, machine install, trials, BIS testing). Only ~6 months of ramping production fall in Year 1, while fixed costs run for the full year.

## Break-Even Analysis

- Contribution per block (at ₹28): ₹28 − ₹15 = **₹13**
- Fixed costs (Year 2): ~₹27 L/year = **₹2.25 L/month**
- Break-even volume: ₹2.25 L ÷ ₹13 = **~17,300 blocks/month** (~700/day)
- Break-even utilisation: 700 ÷ 2,500 = **~28% of capacity**

**Cash break-even is reached around Month 16** — after the ~6-month build and a short ramp.

## Sensitivity Analysis

**Price drops to ₹24/block (deep discount entry):**
- Contribution = ₹24 − ₹15 = ₹9/block
- Break-even = ~25,000 blocks/month (~40% capacity) — still comfortable

**Cement cost rises 30% (₹8.5 → ₹10.5 material):**
- COGS ₹17/block, contribution ₹11 — margin 39%, still healthy

**Year 2 utilisation only 55% (instead of 70%):**
- Volume ~410k blocks, revenue ~₹115 L, net profit ~₹18 L — lower, but still solidly positive
